1. append()

Add text at end

sb.append("Hello");


2. insert()

Insert at specific position

sb.insert(1, "Hi");


3. replace()

Replace part of string

sb.replace(0, 2, "Hi");


4. delete()

Delete characters

sb.delete(0, 2);


5. reverse()

Reverse string

sb.reverse();


6. length()

Get length

sb.length();


7. capacity()

Check storage capacity

sb.capacity();


8. charAt()

Get character

sb.charAt(0);


9. setCharAt()

Change character

sb.setCharAt(0, 'A');


10. toString()

Convert to String

sb.toString();

StringBuilder = fast (not thread-safe)
StringBuffer = slow (thread-safe)
