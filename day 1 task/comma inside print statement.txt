USAGE OF COMMA INSIDE PRINT STATEMENT


1.As format specifier (multiple values)

Comma is used to separate multiple arguments in printf

System.out.printf("Name: %s Age: %d", "John", 25);

Here , separates "John" and 25

2. As thousands separator

Comma is used inside format specifier to format numbers

System.out.printf("%,d", 1000000);

Output:

1,000,000
